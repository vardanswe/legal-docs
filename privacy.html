Privacy Policy

WhoAmI  •  Effective date: 2026-02-22

This Privacy Policy explains how Vardan Galstyan ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the WhoAmI mobile application and related services (the "App").

If you have questions, contact us at vardan.swe@gmail.com.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Account information

When you sign up or sign in, we process your email address and authentication information necessary to provide account access.

1.2 Your inputs and activity

The App allows you to answer prompts and follow-up questions. We store your responses and related in-app events (for example: topic keys, event type, and response content) to provide your results, progress, and product functionality.

1.3 Device and app data

We may process basic device/app information needed to operate the App, such as platform type (iOS/Android/Web) and app version.

1.4 Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may schedule local reminders on your device. At this time, the App does not send push notifications from our servers.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Authenticate users and keep sessions active.
  • Generate in-app insights and progress breakdowns.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and abuse.

3. Service providers (Supabase)

We use Supabase as a backend infrastructure provider (for database storage, authentication, and related services). Supabase processes data on our behalf to operate the App.

4. Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the App (such as Supabase). We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes (such as security, compliance, and support), unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your choices and rights

You can sign out in the App. You can disable notifications in your device settings. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information.

7. Data deletion requests

To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact vardan.swe@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We may need to verify your identity before completing your request.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date and may provide additional notice if changes are material.

10. Contact

For questions or requests, contact us at vardan.swe@gmail.com.
