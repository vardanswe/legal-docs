Common issues

I'm not receiving reminders

  • Check notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Make sure Focus / Do Not Disturb isn't silencing alerts.
  • On Android, verify the app's notification channel is enabled.

I can't sign in

  • Verify you're using the correct email address.
  • Try closing and reopening the app.
  • If the issue persists, email support from your account email.

My data looks wrong or missing

  • Confirm you are signed into the correct account.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Email support with details and the approximate time it happened.

Account and data requests

To request an export or deletion of your account and associated data, email vardan.swe@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

What to include in your message

  • Your account email
  • Device type (iPhone/Android) and OS version
  • App version (if known)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue and screenshots (if possible)
